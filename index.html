Key Objectives Core
Who We Are

Nurturing Tomorrow's Entrepreneurs

We function as a collaborative platform that transforms innovative ideas from students and faculty members into impactful real-world industrial and social solutions.

The Ecosystem

Functioning under the guidance of Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM), the Lourdian Innovation Hub at LMCST provides a highly supportive, technology-enabled environment. We bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice, helping minds explore boundaries and launch scalable ventures.

Vision

“To be an aspirational Technology Business Incubator (TBI) for student entrepreneurs within the campus that strengthens the educational system, bridges the gap between industry and academia, and promotes national, economic, and social growth.”

Mission

“To create a self-sustained ecosystem with adequate infrastructure and technological advancements that empowers interaction between student innovators and faculty mentors, promotes collaboration with industries and academic institutions across the globe, and nurtures successful entrepreneurs.”
Our Pillars

Key Strategic Objectives

Our structured plan is engineered to cover all aspects of startup development, technical skill empowerment, and supportive academic policy.

01

Entrepreneurial Culture

To develop a thriving, proactive entrepreneurial mindset among students, faculty members, and active alumni networks.

02

Innovation & Research

To promote innovation-driven entrepreneurship by integrating student academic projects and advanced research initiatives.

03

Social Impact

To encourage the design, planning, and development of highly innovative products targeting real-world social and industrial relevance.

04

Incubation & Startups

To systematically facilitate seed startup initiatives emerging from creative student minds and experienced faculty members.

05

Empowering Women

To provide targeted guidance, specialized resources, and dedicated startup support systems for aspiring women entrepreneurs.

06

Ecosystem Synergy

To strengthen the collaboration pipelines between modern industry leaders, academic nodes, and the Kerala startup ecosystem.

What We Do

Major Activities & Formats

Our dynamic yearly calendar comprises an array of events and resources built to scale technology and business concepts.

Idea Pitching Sessions

Present, evaluate, and validate your raw innovative business concepts in front of expert juries and receive actionable development advice.

Startup Bootcamps

Immersive, multi-day training camps detailing market fit, target customer profiles, and initial startup operations.

Innovation Workshops

Hands-on collaborative sessions on design thinking, product prototyping cycles, and emerging technological trends.

Hackathons & Ideathons

Competitive high-energy design sprints challenging participants to solve complex social and industrial hurdles in absolute record time.

Technical Skill Programs

Workshops focusing on programming paradigms, web systems, robotics, IoT, AI, and practical engineering skills.

Entrepreneurship Programs (EDP)

Targeted courses detailing legal startup structures, IP management, patent registration, and initial venture planning.

Industry Interactions

Direct dialogue channels and interactive firesides with corporate leaders, technology directors, and industry mentors.

Mentorship & Guidance

Long-term access to specialized technological mentors, financial advisors, and operational business experts.

Prototype Development

Financial grants, computational resources, and physical lab workspace guidance to convert early thoughts into dynamic MVPs.

Startup Networking Events

Exclusive platform sessions to connect with Kerala Startup Mission nodes, venture capitalists, and co-founders.
